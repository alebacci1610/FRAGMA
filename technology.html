Multidimensional Context

The operational architecture of Fragma is not linear; it is vertical. We have mapped the battlefield of reality into a stack of three interlinked planes: the Cognitive, the Virtual, and the Physical. This isometric structure allows for granular control of the perceived environment.

The Cognitive Plane (Levels 1-2) represents the highest order of abstraction. This is where semantic structures, psychological biases, and cultural narratives reside. It is the domain of strategic intent and consensus engineering.

The Virtual Plane acts as the transport layer, utilizing proprietary algorithms to manage signal distribution. Finally, the Physical Plane anchors the system, encompassing the hardware and the biological interface: the human user.
